Rules of the Pig Game:

1 Number of Players:   The game supports 2 to 4 players.

2 Objective: The goal is to be the first player to reach or exceed a specified maximum score, in this case, 50.
Turns: Players take turns rolling a six-sided die.

3 Scoring:
When a player rolls the die:
If they roll a 1, their turn ends, and they lose all points accumulated in that turn.
If they roll any other number, that number is added to their current turn's score.
The player can choose to roll again or end their turn and keep the accumulated points.

4 Game Progression:
Players continue taking turns until one of them reaches the specified maximum score.

5 Winning:
The game ends when a player's total score reaches or exceeds the maximum score.
The player with the highest score at that point is declared the winner.
